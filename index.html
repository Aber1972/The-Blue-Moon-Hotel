About Blue Moon

Blue Moon is a signature landmark hotel combining classical architecture and contemporary hospitality — designed to be equally at home hosting state dinners and private family getaways.

Historic yet contemporary. The original structure was reimagined by a team of international architects to create a layered building that references classical massing while embracing modern material technology. The lobby's double-height atrium serves as the social heart of the hotel, with a curated program of rotating art installations.

Blue Moon maintains a private art collection and partners with local cultural institutions to offer guests exclusive after-hours access and bespoke cultural programming.

Philosophy

We believe great hospitality is both a craft and a science. From the ergonomics of bedding to the acoustics in meeting rooms, every design decision is oriented towards guest comfort and memorable experiences. Training programs for staff emphasize emotional intelligence, discretion and anticipation — the hallmark of five-star service.

Blue Moon is committed to measured sustainability: energy-efficient building systems, water recycling in landscaping, and partnerships with local farmers to reduce food miles.

Architecture, Materials & Interiors

Exterior & Urban Presence

The façade combines limestone plinths with slender vertical fenestration to create a rhythmic urban edge. Night lighting emphasizes cornices and reveals the building's silhouette against the skyline.

Public Interiors

Marble flooring, lacquered millwork, and upholstered walls in the ballroom create an acoustic and tactile richness. Layered lighting systems switch from daylight to an intimate evening palette that transforms the guest journey across hours.

Design Collaborations

Furniture and textiles were commissioned from European ateliers and local craftspeople; select suites include one-off pieces from commissioned artists.

For conservation and operations, the hotel maintains an in-house workshops team for ongoing maintenance and bespoke guest requests (e.g., custom furniture adjustments, artwork framing and installation).

Deluxe Room — Thoughtful Comfort

The Deluxe Room is a careful balance of comfort and efficiency. The king mattress is layered with a choice of soft or medium-firm pillows, 400-thread-count linens and a down duvet. Thoughtful storage, a fully stocked minibar with locally-sourced snacks, and a bathroom fitted with rain shower and heated towel rails create a restorative stay.

Technology: 55" smart TV, high-speed fiber Wi-Fi, Bluetooth soundbar, wireless charging pads.

Guest Services: Turn-down service, express laundry, 24/7 room service.

Executive Suite — For Extended Stays & Work

Designed for travelers who work and rest in equal measure, the Executive Suite separates living and sleeping areas while providing a discrete workspace. The suite is acoustically insulated, includes a Nespresso machine, a curated minibar, and access to the executive lounge at select rates.

Services: Dedicated butler on request, private check-in, optional in-suite dining and meeting setup.

VIP Penthouse — The Ultimate Private Residence

The Penthouse is the hotel's most exclusive offering. With private lift access, a full kitchen, private terrace with plunge pool or jacuzzi, and a dedicated team that includes a personal concierge, housekeeper and chef (on request), the penthouse is ideal for families and heads of delegations who expect privacy and bespoke service.

Penthouse Extras
  • Private arrival experience and welcome amenities
  • Complimentary airport transfer (one round-trip)
  • In-house sommelier and private wine list
  • Customizable in-stay experiences (private screenings, chef's table)

Penthouse bookings include a pre-stay planning call with our concierge team to tailor the suite for arrivals, celebrations, or long-term needs.
